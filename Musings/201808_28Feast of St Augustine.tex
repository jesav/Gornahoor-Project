\section{Feast of St Augustine}

Today is the feast day for \textbf{St Augustine of Hippo}. It is said that \textbf{Plotinus}, \textbf{Augustine}, and \textbf{Boethius} are the intellectual founders of the Middle Ages. If you immerse yourself in them, you will learn much about God, human nature, and the role of Fortuna in your life. Here is one of his prayers:

\begin{verse}
Breathe in me O Holy Ghost, that my thoughts may all be holy.\\
Act in me O Holy Ghost, that my work, too, may be holy.\\
Draw my heart O Holy Ghost, that I love but what is holy.\\
Strengthen me O Holy Ghost, to defend all that is holy.\\
Guard me, then, O Holy Ghost, that I always may be holy.\\
Amen.
\end{verse}
