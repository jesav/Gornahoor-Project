\section{Toil, Pain, Death}

\begin{quotex}
Toil, pain, and death are the great auxiliaries that keep humanity from falling into the bottomless abyss of evil.

\begin{itemize}

\item Through toil, human beings are preserved from irresolute passivity;

\item through pain, they are protected from the danger of excessive subjectivity, because through pain they are reminded of the surrounding world;

\item and through death, they are rescued from a complete severance from the spiritual world.
\end{itemize}

Toil, suffering, and death preserve the human will from passivity, feeling from aloofness, and thinking from obscurity.

\flright{\textsc{Valentin Tomberg}}
\end{quotex}
