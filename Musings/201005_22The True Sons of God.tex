\section{The True Sons of God}

\begin{quotex}
\begin{itemize}

\item The stone exists

\item The plant exists and is living

\item The animal lives and is conscious of its life in its concrete states

\item Man understands the meaning of life according to ideas

\item The sons of God actively realise this meaning of the perfect moral order in all things
\end{itemize}


\flright{\textsc{Vladimir Solovyov}, \emph{The Justification of the Good}}
\end{quotex}

\paragraph{Commentary}

This schema separates Solovyov's system (and \emph{ipso facto} all Traditions) from humanisms. There is a spiritual Kingdom of spiritually aware men that is as different from the worlds of material men as the latter are from mammals.
